DIPLOMATIC CORRESPONDENCE.

SATISFACTION OF THE CHINESE PRINCE AND MINISTERS AT THE KIND RECEPTION
OF CHINESE STUDENTS IN THIS COUNTRY

Minister Low informs the Department of State that the Prince and
Ministers of China have, on several occasions, expressed their
satisfaction at the generous and kind reception of the 30 Chinese
students which left in June last for the United States; and the
sending of the second installment two months earlier than the date
originally fixed on shows that the undertaking has, thus far, more
than met the expectations of its promoters,

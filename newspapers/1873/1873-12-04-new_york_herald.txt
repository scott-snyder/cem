Minister Low informs the Department of State that the Prince and
Ministers of China have on several occasions expressed their
satisfaction at the generous and kind reception of the 80 Chinese
students who left in June last for the United States, and the sending
of the second instalment two months earlier than the date originally
fixed on shows that the undertaking has thus far more than met the
expectations of its promoters.

The chief of the Chinese educational commissioners in New York, the
assistant commissioner and the superintendent in charge of a large
number of Chinese students at Springfield, Mass., arrived in New York
Wednesday, and will spend several days visiting the public
institutions. The visit is made especially for the benefit of the
chief commissioner, who will return to China, where he holds high
rank.

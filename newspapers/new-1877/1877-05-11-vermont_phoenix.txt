The Chinese students In this country are likely to prove formidable
rivals to the American boys. In addition to the facts recently stated
that a Chinese scholar has taken the second prize for declamation in
the Hartford High school, and another the first in the South Hadley
Falls High school, Lee Kwa Pau now takes the first prize for penmanship
in the West Middle public school at Hartford.

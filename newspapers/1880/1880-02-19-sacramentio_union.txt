Mr. Woo Tsze Ting, Secretary of the Chinese Legation at Madrid, lately
appointed Commissioner of the Chinese Educational Mission in Hartford,
Conn., was expected to arrive and assume his office by the beginning
of the Chinese new year. He is said to be a gifted scholar.

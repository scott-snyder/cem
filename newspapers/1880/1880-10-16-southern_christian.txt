—Yun Kwai, the Chinese student in this country who has been ordered
home because of his father’s displeasure at his acceptance of the
Christian religion, will not have to return. The Chinese government
has abandoned all efforts to carry out the decree concerning him. He
has been admitted to Harvard College.

Mr. Woo Tun Ting, Secretary of the Chinese Legation at Madrid, has
been appointed Commissioner of the Chinese Educational Mission at
Hartford, Conn., and is expected to arrive and assume his office by
the beginning of the Chinese new year, which will come next month. He
is said to be a gifted scholar.

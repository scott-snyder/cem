More Chinese students---30 of them---are expected to arrive soon, on
their way to school in Massachusetts and Connecticut. Those who have
been studying in New England for the past two years are fitting for
our higher grades of schools, and will enter our scientific schools
and colleges as soon as they are prepared to do so.

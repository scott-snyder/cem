Charles Laisun, known at Hamilton College in 1847-8 as a a Chinese boy
of eighteen, and a student preparing for military service, is reported
by the Utica Herald as now visiting Clinton, N. Y. He now ranks as a
second mandarin, and. with his wife, a Chinese lady, and three sons
and three daughters will settle in Springfield, Mass.

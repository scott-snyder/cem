The thirty Chinese students coming here to be educated are described
as ladies and gentlemen, very young and very fair, a sort of prize lot
the like of which have never been here before. The Chinese government
only allows the entire thirty one million of dollars for their
education. They may squeeze through on $33,000 each, but it will
require rigid economy.

Title: CHINESE EDUCATIONAL MISSION.
Author: 
---

Advices from China indicate the recall of the Chinese educational
mission, established at Hartford in 1872. There are about 100 young
Chinese men studying in this country, of whom about 50 are in colleges
and technical schools.

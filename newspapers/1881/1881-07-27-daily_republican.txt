The Cheyenne Leader publishes a report of an interview with Tsu Soon
Kee, an attache of the Chinese Legation at Washington, who says that
the Chinese students withdrawn from America will be sent to England,
France and Germany, and hereafter the Chinese Government "will educate
students in Europe and also in China, where military and naval schools
are being established."

The Chinese students who arrived here yesterday to be educated in
America, are evidently of a high class. They are much lighter
complexloned than any of their countrymen here, far more delicate
featured and intelligent looking and are richly dressed. They are
mostly mere boys, but very refined and gentlemanly in manners.

Mother Goose has got after the Chinese
students for Yale, thus:

  Thirty Heathen Chinee
    Riding on a rail;
  Thirty long-tailed Freshman
    Coming down to Yale.
  Should they read Confucius
    Instead of morning prayers,
  We'll take them by the hind legs
    And throw them down stairs.

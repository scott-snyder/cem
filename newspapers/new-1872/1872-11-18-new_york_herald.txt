Charles Laisun, well known to Auburnians at Hamilton College in 1847-8
as a Chinese boy of eighteen and a student preparing for missionary
service, is reported by the Utica Herald as now visiting at
Clinton. He now ranks as a second mandarin, and, with his wife, a
Chinese lady, and three sons and three daughters, will settle In
Springfield, Mass.

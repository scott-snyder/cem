As the last Chinese students, while in this country, became
contaminated by Christianity, the education of these who have just
arrived in San Francisco will be carefully watched over, all the forms
of the religion of Confucius will be rigidly enforced, and four books
of the Five Kings and the sacred edict of Emperor Kangshi will be
portions of their regular lessons.

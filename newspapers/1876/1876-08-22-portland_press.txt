Title: Chinese Visitors to the Centennial
Author:

Hartford, Conn., Aug. 21â€“Eighty Chinese boys left here this noon for
the Centennial. They will be joined at New Haven by thirty more. They
are the Chinese students who are being educated in this country under
the superintendence of the Chinese Educational Commission. They are
accompanied by Kwon Ki, a Chinese interpreter, and Len Ki Cheun, a
teacher, and Prof. Bartlett of the American Asylum here, Prof. Carlton
of the State Normal School, Prof. Northrop of the Connecticut
Educational Board, and William H. Kellogg. They will stay in
Philadelphia until Friday. The Chinese Commissioners will visit the
exhibition later.

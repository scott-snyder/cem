California sends eighty Chinese boys to the Centennial. They are
students, being educated in this country under the superintendence of
the Chinese educational commission. If they can be educated to demand
and receive adequate compensation for their labor, some good may be
done, but we doubt whether this national characteristic can be
eradicated by any such means.

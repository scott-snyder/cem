Hartford, Conn.; Aug. 21---Eighty Chinese boys left here this noon for
Philadelphia to attend the Exhibition. At New Haven they will be
joined by about thirty other Chinese boys.  They are the students
being educated in this country under the superintendence of the
Chinese educational commission. They are accompanied by Kwang Ki-Chin,
Interpreter; Leu Ki-Chenn, teacher; Prof. De Bartlett, of the American
asylum here: Prof. J.N. Carle, of the State Normal school;
Prof. B.G. Northrop, of the Connecticut Educational Board, and
Wm.H. Kellogg. They will stay in Philadelphia until Friday. The
Chinese commissioner will visit the Exhibition later.

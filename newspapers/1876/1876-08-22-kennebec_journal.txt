CONNECTICUT.

The Centennial.

Hartford, August 21. Eighty Chinese left here this noon for the
Centennial. They will be joined at New Haven by thirty more. They are
the Chinese students being educated in this country.  They are
accompanied by Kivony Ki Chin as interpreter, Leli Ki Cheun, teacher,
and Prof. Bartlett of the American Asylum here, and Prof Carleton of
the State normal school, Prof. Northend of Connecticut Educational
Board, and Wm. H.  Kellogg. They will stay in Philadelphia until
Friday. The Chinese commissioners will visit the exhibition later.

A VISIT FROM CHINESE STUDENTS,

Hartford, Conn, Aug. 21.â€”Eighty Chinese boys left here this noon for
Philadelphia to attend the Exhibition. At New-Haven they were joined
by about thirty others. These students are being educated in this
country under the superintendence of the Chinese Educational
Commission.  They are accompanied to Philadelphia by Kwang Ki Chin,
interpreter; Len Ki Cheun, teacher; Prof. D.E. Bartlett of the
American Asylum in this city; Prof. J.N. Carleton of the State Normal
school; Prof. B.G. Northrop of the Connecticut Educational Board, and
Wm.H. Kellogg, They will stay in Philadelphia until Friday. The
Chinese Commissioners will visit the Exhibition later.

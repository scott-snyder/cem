The chief of the Chinese educational commissioners in this city, the
assistant commissioner and the superintendent in charge at a large
number of Chinese students at Springfield, Mass., arrived yesterday
morning, and will spend several days visiting public institutions. The
visit is made especially for the benefit of the chief commissioner,
who will soon return to China, where be holds high rank.

CHINESE APPRECIATION.

Minister Low states that the prince and ministers of China have, on
several occasions, expressed satisfaction at the generous reception of
the thirty Chinese students in the United States, and the sending of a
second installment two months earlier than the date originally fixed
on shows that the undertaking has thus far more than met the
expectations of its promoters.

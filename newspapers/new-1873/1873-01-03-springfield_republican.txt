Gen Hawley and wife joined the Asylum Hill Congregational church at
Hartford, Sunday, the former by profession and the latter by letter
from Christ Episcopal church. Among the others received into the
church at the same time was Lo-a-Yip, the Chinese tailor employed by
the commissioner in charge of the students from that country at school
in Connecticnt, who brought a letter from the Chinese church at Hong
Kong, of which he was for some years a member.

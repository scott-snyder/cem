FRIENDLY RELATIONS WITH CHINA.

Minister Low informs the Department of Slate that the Prince and
Ministers of China have on several occasions expressed their
satisfaction at the generous and kind reception of the thirty Chinese
students who left in June last for tho United States, and the sending
of tho second installment two months earlier than tho date originally
fixed on shown that the undertaking has thus far more than met the
expectations of its promoters.

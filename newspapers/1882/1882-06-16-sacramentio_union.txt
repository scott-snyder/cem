Chinese Students to be Returned.

Denver (Col.), June 15th. Chang Tsung Liang, an attache of the Chinese
Legation, passed through to-day on his way to Washington. He says the
Chinese Government will return fifty students to American colleges,
having discovered that their removal was a mistake.

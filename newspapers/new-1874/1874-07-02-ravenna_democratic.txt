Thirty more Chinese students are expected to arrive in this country
soon, on their way to schools in Massachusetts and Connecticut. Those
who have been studying in New England for the past two years are all
fitting for higher grades of schools, and will enter scientific
schools and colleges as soon as they are prepared to do so.

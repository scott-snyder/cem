reception to the trustees of the University of Modern Languages here
established, and City Hall is crowded with ladies and gentlemen, Mayor
Currier presiding. Eloquent and earnest addresses have been made by

Portland, Rev. Dr. Stockbridge of Providence, and Chan Larsan, Chinese
Commissioner of Education; by Senator Patterson of New Hampshire, and

the country, and at the opening this fall students are coming from
China, Japan, Mexico, and various other countries.

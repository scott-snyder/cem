Presidential Party.

Philadelphia, August 24.  President Grant accompanied by Mrs. Grant,
Mr. and Miss Sharp, U.S. Grant, jr., and Gen Dent, arrived from Long
Branch this morning, and were received by Gen. Hawley and Director
Gen. Gashorn. The President spent several hours in Machinery Hall and
before leaving the grounds received the Chinese students in Judges'
Hall.

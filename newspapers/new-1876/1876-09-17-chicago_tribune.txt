The Chinese students of Hartford have formed a base-ball club which
they have named the Oriental Base-Ball Club. It is pleasant for the
scorer to call each names as these to the bat: Sy Chung, Kwal Pau,
Kee Jung, Shen Chi, and Tun Ven.

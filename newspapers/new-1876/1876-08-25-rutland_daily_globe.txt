The Centennial.

THE PRESIDENT IN ATTENDANCE.

Philadelphia, Aug. 24.

President Grant, accompanied by Mr.  Grant, Mr. and Miss Sharp,
U.S. Grant, jr., and Gen. Dent, arrived here from Long Branch this
morning. The party was received by Gen. Hawley and Director
Gen. Goshorn.  The president spent several hours in machinery hall and
before leaving the grounds received the Chinese students in the
judges' hall.

THE PRESIDENT AND FAMILY AT THE CENTENNIAL.

Philadelphia, August 24.---President Grant, accompanied by Mrs. Grant,
Mr. and Mrs. Sharpe, U.S. Grant Jr., and General Dent, arrived here
from Long Branch this morning.  The President spent several hours in
Machinery Hall, and before leaving the grounds received the Chinese
students in the judges' hall.

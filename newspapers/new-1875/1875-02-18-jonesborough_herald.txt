There are now sixty Chinese students, supported by their government,
in the schools of Massachusetts and Connecticut, and thirty more are
expected in a few weeks. On their arrival they were placed two by two,
in cultivated families, where their first aim is the mastery of our
language. Their progress is said to be quite remarkable, and their
deportment such as to make them general favorites.

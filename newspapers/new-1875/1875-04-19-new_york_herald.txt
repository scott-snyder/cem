TSAU-KIA-TSIOH, CHINESE STUDENT.

Tsau-Kia-Tsioh, a Chinese student, twelve years old, one of the
company of Asiatics who arrived in the United States last November,
died at the residence of the Chinese Commissioner, in Hartford, Conn.,
during the night of the 17th inst. He had been under instruction with
a family in North Amherst, Mass.

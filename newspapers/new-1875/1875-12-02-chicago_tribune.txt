Thirty-eight Chinese students, on route to colleges in New England,
have just arrived from San Francisco, via tho Chicago & Northwestern
Railway. This is the fourth lot that the Government of China have sent
here to be educated.  The party consisted of boys from 3 to 15
years old, who are to remain East fifteen years.

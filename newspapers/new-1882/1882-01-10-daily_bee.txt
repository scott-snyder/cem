A Chinese Student Beheaded-
National Associated Press.

NEW HAVEN Conn., January 9---Advices received here from Hong Kong
state that Chin Chin Chan, a Chinese student who left this city where
he was fitting for Yale college and returned to China last summer has
been beheaded because he carried on a correspondence with a young lady
in this city. All Chinese students wore recalled because the
authorities found they were becoming Americanized.

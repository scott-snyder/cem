Chin Han Pin, the new Ambassador to the United States from China, is
accompanied by a Consular corps accredited to Cuba, Peru and San
Francisco. Chin Han Pin is a highly educated and distinguished
diplomat and statesman, and has been Commissioner to several
countries. He visited this country several years ago to arrange for
the education of Chinese students. He will remain about ten years at
Washington.

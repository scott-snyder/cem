Chen Lan Pin, Embassabor to the United States from China, and suite,
will arrive here in the City of Tokio, due on the 18th. He is
accompanied by a Consular corps, accredited to Cuba, Peru and this
city. Chen Lan Pin is highly educated and is a distinguished diplomat
and statesman, and has been a Commissioner to several foreign
countries.  He visited this country some years ago, to arrange for the
education of Chinese students.  He will remain here about ten days
before proceeding to Washington.

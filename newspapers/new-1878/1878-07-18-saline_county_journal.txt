Chen Han Pin, Ambassador to the United States from China, was to
arrive with his suite in the steamship City of Tokio, due on the 18th
inst. He is accompanied by a Consular corps accredited to Cuba, Peru,
and California.  Chen Han Pin is a highly educated and distinguished
diplomat and statesman, and has been Commissioner to several foreign
countries. He visited this country some years ago to arrange for the
education of Chinese students.

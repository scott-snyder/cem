THE CHINESE EMBASSY, consisting of 45 persons, including servants,
passed through Omaha, coming east.  Wednesday afternoon on a special
car.  His excellency Chin Lan Pin, chief of embassy, came to the United
States in 1872 as chief commissioner in charge of the Chinese students
sent to the New England colleges to be educated, and afterward visited
England and Spain. He will establish number of consulates in the
United States and then go to Spain on business concerning a consulate
to Cuba, and afterward to Peru on a similar mission. He will thence
return to Washington, where he will take up his abode as minister
resident. The embassy will visit the schools in New England where the
Chinese pupils are receiving their education before coming to
Washington the first time.


The Chinese Embassy.

BUFFALO, August 9---The Chinese embassy passed through this city at
noon to-day.

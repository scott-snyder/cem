CHEN HAN PIN EXPECTED NEXT WEEK.

San Francisco, July 12.â€”Chen Han Pin, Ambassador to the United States
from China, will arrive here with his suite in the steamship City of
Tokio, due on the 18th inst. He is accompanied by a consular corps
accredited to Cuba, Peru and this city. Chen Han Pin is a highly
educated and distinguished diplomat and statesman, and has been
commissioner to several foreign countries. He visited this country
several years ago to arrange for the education of Chinese students. He
will remain here about ten days before proceeding to Washington.

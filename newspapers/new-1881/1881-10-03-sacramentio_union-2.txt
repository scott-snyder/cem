Carlin, October 21.---Passed here to-day, to arrive in Sacramento
to-morrow: H. Wadsworth, James H. Pierce and wife,
L. B. Seeley. Mrs. Lena Nathan, H. Crandall, Miss A. Brown, San
Francisco; William H.  Scott, Jr., New York; Mrs. L. H. Olmsted, Miss
Olmsted, E. Packard, Oakland; Mrs.  M. L. Stuart, Mrs. B. M. Stuart,
Conn.; Mrs. B. C. Cavellier, Florida; Mrs. A.  Robehen, Nova Scotia;
Mrs. E. Seawell, St.  Johns; N. B. D. Green, Salt Lake; R. M.
McChell, Brooklyn, N. Y.; B. F. Hall, Arizona; Mrs. E. Crabbs, Ohio;
William Chambers, A. R. Shaw, Mrs. Shaw, England; Mrs. E. E. Howe,
Pittsburg, Pa.; Mrs. Nye, Hollister, Cal.; P. J. Sullivan,
C. F. Logan, Eagle Rock, Idaho; James Maguire, Omaha; George
Ingersoll, Chicago; Miss Andrews, Mrs. Andrews, Mr. Andrews, Atlanta,
Ga. Also, 25 Chinese students; 52 emigrant passengers, including 30
males, to arrive in Sacramento October 4th.

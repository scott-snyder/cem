Chin Tah Fay, a Chinese student in the family Of Mr. Charles Phelps,
of Hebron, Conn., about two years ago was sent back to China by the
Commissioners for general misconduct. A letter received a few days ago
from one of his follow-students stated that Mr Chin had since his
return to China displayed his ferocious disposition by murdering his
father, for which act he has been beheaded. He was while in this
country a terror to the family with which he lived.

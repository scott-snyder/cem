Too Liberal.—Young Chinamen, of good family, will no longer be
permitted to come to this country as students. The Chinese government
has discovered that these young men return home with liberal views on
many matters, and as that government is one of the most despotic in
the world, it has no mercy for those who would introduce
innovations. But notwithstanding the prohibition against American and
European education of Chinese youths, the wheels of progress can’t be
turned back.  The world will continue to move, even in China.

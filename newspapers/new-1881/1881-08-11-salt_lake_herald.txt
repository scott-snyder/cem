The Chinese Students.

Chicago 10.--Twenty-five Chinese students on the way from Hartford
home arrived here today. They say they have completed the studies for
which they were sent. One reason they gave for being summoned home is
that government's military and naval academies are closed to them. As
Germany allows them to enter her institutions China will probably send
her students there and to England. They state that none of their party
have changed their faith in America. They were well dressed, spoke
English well, and were a creditable looking set of men

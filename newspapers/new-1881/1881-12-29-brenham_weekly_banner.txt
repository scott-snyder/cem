The sudden withdrawal of the Chinese students from our colleges has
given rise to many surmises at Washington and in educational
circles. The fact that they are not ordered home, but are to be
transferred to European colleges, has naturally created the impression
that their removal is intended as a retaliating measure to offset the
undisguised anti-Chinese sentiment that was so strongly developed,
particularly in the west, last year.

Colombia has set a good example to the Spanish-American nations in
deciding to send workmen abroad to acquire a knowledge of the
mechanical arts, in order that they may instruct others on their
return. The same principle is applied more systematically in the
Chinese Educational Mission, but the resources and educational zeal of
Colombia are not yet commensurate with an undertaking of that extent.

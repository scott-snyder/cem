The Chinese Ambassador.

San Francisco, July 12.--Chen Han Pin, Ambassador to the United
States* from China, will arrive here with his suit in the City of
Tokio, due on the 18th instant. He is accompanied by a consular corps,
accredited to Cuba, Peru, and this city.  Chen Han Pin is highly
educated, and is a distinguished diplomat and statesman, and has been
commissioned to several foreign countries. He visited this country
several years ago to arrange for the education of Chinese students. He
will remain here about ten days before proceeding to Washington.

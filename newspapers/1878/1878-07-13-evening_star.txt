Chen Han Pin, ambassador to the United States from China, will arrive
in San Francisco with his suite in the steamship City of Tokio, due on
the 18th inst., and will remain in California about two weeks before
coming to Washington. He is accompanied by a consular corps accredited
to Cuba, Peru and San Francisco. He visited this country some years
ago to arrange for the education of Chinese students.

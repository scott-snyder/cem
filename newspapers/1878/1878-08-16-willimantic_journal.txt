Rev. A. J. Sullivan is absent on a vacation of four weeks. A number of
Chinese students are visiting with their former teachers prior to
entering other schools, some will go to Hartford, some to New Haven
and others to Glastonbury. R. S.  Bestor is to move to New Haven next
week to board those that have been taught by his wife.
